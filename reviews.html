Wicked (2024)

★★★★☆

Wicked delivers the emotional punch and visual splendor fans have waited for, even if it stumbles slightly under its own ambition. Bring tissues—you'll laugh, cry, and leave humming every song.

Best For: Musical lovers, Wizard of Oz fans, and anyone who needs a reminder that "no one mourns the wicked" until they know their story.


The Substance

★★★★½

Think Black Swan meets The Fly by way of Showgirls. Fargeat's film is a midnight movie with brains, bravado, and buckets of blood. Not for everyone, but unforgettable for those who can stomach it.

Best For: Horror fans, cinephiles, and anyone who's ever feared a mirror


Oppenheimer

★★★★★

More than just a history lesson, Oppenheimer is a profoundly unsettling examination of scientific ethics that lingers long after viewing. Nolan's best work since The Dark Knight.

Best For: Fans of intelligent cinema, history buffs, and anyone who appreciates acting masterclasses.
